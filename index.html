Healthy Living

One Major way of maintaining a health lifestyle is by watching was you eat.

What we take into our bodies and when we take it impact our body function quite alot.
One can maintain a healthy lifestyle by adhering to a proper balance diet.

Balanced Diet

A proper balanced diet should include the following.

  • Protein
  • Carbohydrates
  • Fats & Oils
  • Mineral Salts
  • Vitamins(Fruits & Vegetables)
  • Water
